Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about our Sahara bivouac camps, camel treks, inclusions, and practical tips for your Trek Desert Morocco experience.

Reserve directly on our site or via Booking/WhatsApp. We confirm within a few hours. A small deposit secures your date; the balance is paid on arrival (cash, card in Merzouga, or bank transfer in advance).
Private tent (standard or luxury), dinner & breakfast, evening campfire (weather-permitting), mint tea, sand-boarding, and sunrise dunes walk. Transfers by 4×4 or camel from the meeting point are available—see your package.
All tents are private unless you request a shared option. Luxury tents feature en-suite bathroom and king/queen beds. Standard tents use shared, clean facilities a short walk away.
Yes! We welcome families. Children under 6 should ride with an adult or take 4×4 transfer. We can provide extra beds and earlier dinner times on request.
Closed shoes, warm layers (nights can be cold Oct–Mar), hat/scarf, sunscreen, sunglasses, reusable water bottle, small backpack. We’ll store your suitcase at the meeting point.
Of course—please tell us in advance. We regularly cook vegetarian and gluten-free meals. Severe allergies: we’ll share ingredients and adapt the menu.
Luxury tents include private en-suite with hot shower and WC. Standard camps offer clean shared facilities. Solar power is available for lighting and phone charging (bring a power bank just in case).
We meet in Merzouga/Erg Chebbi 1–2 hours before sunset. You can leave your car safely, then ride camel or 4×4 to the camp. Private transfers from Erfoud, Rissani, or desert hotels can be arranged.
Free date change subject to availability. Full refund up to 72h before arrival; 50% within 72–24h; no-show is non-refundable. If weather makes the trek unsafe, we offer alternatives or a full refund.
Our camels are well cared for and the route is paced and guided. Helmets are available on request. If you prefer not to ride, a 4×4 transfer reaches the camp by dunes track.
